Cookies

Cookies are very delicious. It makes a lot of people want to buy them.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (226g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (220g) brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups (360g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 0.5 tsp baking powder
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 2 cups (340g) chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Cream Butter and Sugar: In a large bowl, beat the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Add Eggs and Vanilla: Add the eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract. Mix until combined.
  4. Combine Dry Ingredients: In another bowl, mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add this mixture to the wet ingredients.
  5. Fold in Chocolate Chips: Gently fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop and Bake: Use a cookie scoop or tablespoon to place dough on the baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Cool and Enjoy: Allow cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
