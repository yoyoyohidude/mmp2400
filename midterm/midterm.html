The West Wing

2015, TV-14, 7 Seasons

Step into the heart of American politics with this groundbreaking drama series from producer Aaron Sorkin. At its center is Josiah Bartlet, a sharp and charismatic U.S. President whose down-to-earth demeanor and country-lawyer sensibility are matched only by his unwavering dedication to doing what's best for the nation.
